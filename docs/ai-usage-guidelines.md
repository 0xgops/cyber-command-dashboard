# AI Usage Guidelines

This is how I use AI to support my learning while keeping my work honest and original.

### How I Use AI
- To break down difficult concepts  
- To get explanations in plain language  
- To help plan assignments or brainstorm ideas  
- To practice, quiz myself, or check my understanding  
- To improve clarity and structure in my writing after I’ve already drafted it  
- To review code, understand errors, or get examples  

### What I Do Myself
- All final writing  
- All graded assignments  
- All analysis, arguments, and conclusions  
- All lab work and hands-on tasks  
- Any code I submit  

### What I Don’t Use AI For
- Closed-book exams  
- Timed quizzes  
- Assignments labeled “no outside help”  
- Copy-pasting answers  

### If I Mention AI in an Assignment
I’ll say something simple like:

> “I used AI to clarify concepts and to help organize my outline. All writing and final analysis are my own.”

This keeps things transparent without giving the impression that the AI produced the assignment.
