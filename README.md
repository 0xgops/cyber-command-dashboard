# Canton Cyber Degree – Greg Lawson

This repo is my personal system for organizing everything related to my Cybersecurity degree at SUNY Canton.  
It holds my notes, lab work, assignments, study routines, and long-term planning. My goal is to keep everything in one place so I can look back and see my growth semester by semester.

### What’s Inside
- **docs/** – planning files, study systems, and guidelines  
- **templates/** – note, lab, and assignment templates I reuse  
- **semester-01-Spring-2026/** – all course material for my first term  
- **scripts/** – helper tools as I build them  
- **web-interface/** – a simple dashboard for quick navigation  
- **archive/** – older files I want to keep but not front-and-center  

### Purpose
If I ever needed to re-create my whole academic workflow, this repo would let me do it.  
It’s also a way for me to stay consistent, disciplined, and build a record of my progress as I move toward a career in cybersecurity.
