# SOCI 101 – Intro to Sociology

This class covers how society works, how people interact, and how social structures shape behavior.  
It helps build writing, analysis, and critical thinking skills.

## What I’ll Focus On
- Key sociological concepts and theories  
- How groups, cultures, and institutions influence people  
- Social patterns and inequalities  
- Connecting sociology to everyday life and current issues  

## Folder Layout
- **notes/** – chapter breakdowns, lecture summaries, definitions  
- **assignments/** – reflections, essays, and discussion responses  
- **resources/** – charts, articles, and anything that helps with understanding  

## Goals for the Semester
- Strengthen academic writing and critical thinking  
- Pull out the meaningful ideas, not memorize everything  
- Keep clean, organized notes I can quickly review
