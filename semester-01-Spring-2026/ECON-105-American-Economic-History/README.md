# ECON 105 – American Economic History

This folder holds my notes, readings, and assignments for ECON 105.  
The goal of this class is to understand how the U.S. economy developed, what shaped it, and how past events connect to the present.

## What I’ll Focus On
- Major turning points in American economic development  
- How policy, technology, and social changes shaped growth  
- Patterns and cycles in the U.S. economy  
- Understanding economic history without memorizing everything  

## How I’ll Use This Folder
- **notes/** – summaries of lectures and readings  
- **assignments/** – written work, reflections, and essays  
- **resources/** – charts, timelines, outside readings  

## Goals for the Semester
- Build clear, organized notes that I can return to later  
- Strengthen writing for humanities-style assignments  
- Connect what I learn here with modern economics
