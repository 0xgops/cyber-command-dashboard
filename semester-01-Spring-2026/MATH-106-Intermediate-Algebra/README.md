# MATH 106 – Intermediate Algebra
This folder holds my practice sets, formula sheets, and exam reviews for MATH 106.
The goal of this course is to bridge the gap between basic algebra and pre-calculus by mastering functions, graphing, and complex problem-solving.
What I’ll Focus On
 * Functions & Graphs – Linear, quadratic, and exponential models.
 * Systems of Equations – Solving for multiple variables efficiently.
 * Radicals & Rationals – Simplifying complex expressions and working with x^{1/n} notation.
 * Test Readiness – Focusing on the process of the solution, not just the final answer.
How I’ll Use This Folder
 * practice/ – Scans of handwritten problem sets and digital worksheets.
 * formulas/ – Quick-reference sheets for theorems and common identities.
 * reviews/ – Summary sheets for midterms and the final exam.
Goals for the Semester
 * Master the quadratic formula x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} until it's second nature.
 * Reduce simple calculation errors through consistent practice.
 * Maintain a 1:1 ratio of lecture time to independent practice.
